## Why this matters

The UN system spends more than **$2 billion annually** on ICT. A baseline across **28 entities** (covering **97%** of expenditure) confirms significant **overlap in core services** and **interoperability challenges**. Work Package 15 addresses this through **Action 61** (ICT consolidation) and **Action 62** (Technology Accelerator Platform), with public secondary evidence now monitored on a recurring automated basis.

## Our goal

Reduce duplication, improve interoperability, and accelerate responsible digital innovation — while keeping a clear **human review gate** on all outputs derived from this research hub.
